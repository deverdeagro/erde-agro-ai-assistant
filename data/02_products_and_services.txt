ERDE Agro Products and Services


1. Farm Monitoring Platform

Description:

ERDE Agro provides technology-based farm monitoring solutions using satellite data and agriculture intelligence.

Features:

- Crop monitoring
- Farm health analysis
- Weather insights
- Water management support
- Crop growth tracking


2. Precision Agriculture

Description:

Precision agriculture helps farmers make decisions using data instead of assumptions.

Benefits:

- Better resource usage
- Improved productivity
- Reduced wastage
- Sustainable farming


3. Intelligent Irrigation Support

Description:

Technology-based irrigation guidance helps farmers optimize water usage.

Features:

- Soil moisture monitoring
- Crop water requirement analysis
- Irrigation recommendations


4. Soil Testing Services

Description:

ERDE Agro supports soil testing and soil health improvement.

Benefits:

- Better fertilizer planning
- Improved soil management
- Higher crop efficiency


5. Market Linkage

Description:

ERDE Agro helps connect farmers and buyers.

Benefits:

For Farmers:
- Better price discovery
- Direct buyer connection
- Transparent process

For Buyers:
- Reliable supply
- Quality assurance
- Traceability


6. Contract Farming Support

Description:

ERDE Agro supports structured farming programs where farmers and buyers work together based on agreed crop requirements.


7. Input Support

Services include:

- Agricultural inputs
- Crop advisory
- Farming best practices
