Company Name:
ERDE Agro Ecosystems Pvt Ltd

About Company:

ERDE Agro Ecosystems is an agriculture technology and ecosystem company working towards creating a transparent and sustainable agricultural value chain.

The company focuses on connecting farmers, buyers, technology providers, and ecosystem partners to improve farming practices, increase transparency, and create better market opportunities.

ERDE Agro works towards helping farmers move towards precision farming, residue-free farming, and sustainable agriculture practices.

Mission:

To bridge the gap between farmers and end-users by enabling better farming practices, reliable supply chains, and transparent market connections.

Vision:

To build a sustainable agriculture ecosystem where farmers, buyers, and technology partners benefit together.

Core Areas:

1. Farmer Support
2. Precision Agriculture
3. Market Linkage
4. Contract Farming
5. Supply Chain Management
6. Sustainable Farming


Geographical Presence:

ERDE Agro works with agriculture ecosystems across multiple regions of India including Maharashtra, Gujarat, Goa, Telangana, and Andhra Pradesh.

The company has also explored international agriculture projects.

Head Office:

Mumbai, Maharashtra, India


Business Model:

ERDE Agro creates connections between:

Farmers → Technology → Buyers

The company helps farmers improve productivity and connects quality agricultural produce with commercial buyers.
